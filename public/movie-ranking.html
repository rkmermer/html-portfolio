Top Films of 2024

My top 3 films of last year.

1. Nosferatu

An absolutely stunning remake of a classic horror film. Phenomenal visual and sound design give so much atmosphere and the performances are deserving of awards.

2. The Substance

One of the most surprising and enjoyable films I've seen in a long time. great if you enjoy body horror and a little bit of silly fun.

3. Dune - Part 2

An achievment in Sci-Fi cinema!
